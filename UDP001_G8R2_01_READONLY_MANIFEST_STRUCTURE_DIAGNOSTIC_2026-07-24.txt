3. recursively flatten JSON leaf paths;
4. compare leaf-path sets;
5. compare leaf values;
6. classify every differing leaf into:

- PATH
  Absolute/local directory differs. Compare the basename separately.

- TIMESTAMP
  Creation/start/end/run timestamp differs.

- DURATION
  Elapsed/runtime/performance duration differs.

- ARTIFACT_HASH
  Hash/fingerprint of a run-generated intermediate differs.

- ORDER_ONLY
  Dictionary/list order differs but normalized semantic content is equal.

- VALUE_FORMAT_ONLY
  Numeric/string formatting differs but parsed value is equal.

- OTHER
  Any difference not proven to belong to the above categories.

Do not delete or normalize a leaf during this diagnostic. Classification is
reporting only.

For PATH:

- equal basename with different parent path is PATH;
- different basename is OTHER.

For numeric values:

- 1, 1.0, and "1" may be reported VALUE_FORMAT_ONLY only when exact numeric
  conversion proves equality;
- false/0, blank/0, and unavailable/0 are not automatically equal.
